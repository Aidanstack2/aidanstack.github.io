Personal portfolio site — NYC urban policy and fiscal analysis projects
# Aidan Stack
MS Urban Policy and Leadership, CUNY Hunter College

Data analytics and policy analysis focused on NYC public sector fiscal systems, 
budget performance, and procurement equity.

## Projects

**NYC Agency Budget Performance Analysis**
Analysis of adopted vs. actual spending for DOE, ACS, and DSS across FY2020–2024 
using OMB financial plan data. Examines budget variance patterns and relationship 
to service outcomes including graduation rates and shelter census.
[View Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/views/NYCAgencyBudgetAnalysis/CombinedDashboards?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)

**NYC Agency Procurement and M/WBE Equity Analysis**
Analysis of contract award patterns across DOE, ACS, and DSS using NYC Comptroller 
Checkbook NYC data. Examines M/WBE vendor utilization by category and award method 
across FY2020–2024.
[View Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/views/NYCAgencyProcurementandMWBEEquityAnalysisFY20202024/Dashboard1?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)
